TurtleCorp Studio, a small group of students from ELAM, in East London. Our new game that is coming out is Wars Ruin. A game focusing on the recovery from a war and the problems caused by it. This game will not only be about a daring adventure in a realistic war torn world, but also about an educational, meaningful story about the impacts of war. You will be playing as an abandoned soldier in a hostile and damaged environment, full of distrust and hate. You will be on a journey to find a purpose in life when all is lost and facing your greatest fears. Follow us on this quest of completing this game!
